Naming conventions:
	XML: snake_case (e.g. layout_custom)	
	Java: camelCase
		class:     starting with capital letter (e.g. MyClass.class)
		variable: no capital (e.g. myVariable)
		consts : all caps, divided with underscores (e.g. NOTIFICATION_TITLE_KEY)



	widgets: widget type at beginning of ID (e.g. for a Button btn_mybutton in xml, btnMybutton in java)
		for shortname of widgets see list below


Button: btn
TextView: txt
EditText: edt
FloatingActionButton: fbtn
